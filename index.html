MAIN CHARACTER

V is the main protagonist of the game and is controlled by the player.
V's appearance, gender, and more are fully customizable.

Name V
Gender Player-determined
Base Night City
Occupation Mercenary
Voice Actor Gavin Drea / Cherami Leigh

Role in the Story

V is the playable mercenary who Cyberpunk 2077's plot revolves around.
The digitized consciousness of Johnny Silverhand get embedded into V's brain after stealing a biochip. As the story progresses, V must choose whether to side with Johnny against the Arasaka Corportation or defy him.

Backstory

V's backstory and lifepath history are entirely dependent on the Character Customization the player chooses. Choosing V's Lifepath history, as either being Nomad, Street Kid, or Corporate, has deep impact on the game's plot.

Gameplay

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world, action-adventure story set in Night City, a megalopolis obsessed with power, glamour and body modification. You play as V, a mercenary outlaw going after a one-of-a-kind implant that is the key to immortality. You can customize your character’s cyberware, skillset and playstyle, and explore a vast city where the choices you make shape the story and the world around you.
